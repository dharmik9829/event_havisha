About us

Welcome to Havisha Events, a leading event management company dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for our clients. We specialize in planning and executing a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate events, and celebrity events.

At Havisha Events, we understand that planning an event can be a daunting task, which is why we are here to help. Our team of experienced event planners will work with you from start to finish to ensure that your event is a success. We take the time to get to know our clients and their needs, so that we can create an event that truly reflects their vision and achieves their goals.

We are Havisha Events pride ourselves on our attention to detail, creativity, and personalized service. We understand that every event is unique and requires a tailored approach. That's why we offer a variety of services to meet the specific needs of each of our clients, from full-service event planning to day-of coordination.
